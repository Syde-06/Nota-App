Features

Everything you need to
study smarter

📥
Import PDF & DOCX
Upload any document up to 50 MB. NOTA extracts the full text and makes it ready for highlighting.
🎨
Color-role highlighting
Five semantic colors — Title, Definition, List, Example, Summary — turn raw highlights into structure.
📤

How it works

From document to knowledge
in three steps

1
Import your document
Upload a PDF or DOCX file from your device. NOTA stores it securely and extracts all readable text automatically.
2
Highlight by color role
Open the Highlight Workspace and assign each section a semantic color — red for titles, yellow for definitions, green for lists, blue for examples, and purple for summaries.
3
Preview, enhance, and export
Review the structured live preview, optionally run AI enhancement, then export to your preferred format — PDF, Word, Markdown, Notion, or Obsidian.
